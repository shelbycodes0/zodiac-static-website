What is the Chinese Zodiac?

Chinese astrology has 12 zodiac signs - represented by animals.
Your sign is determined by what year you were born.
Chinese astrology has a rich history of more than 2,000 years.
The zodiac animals are believed to influence personality, career, compatability, relationships and wealth.

There is an amusing legend about the origin of Chinese zodiac. The Jade Emperor wanted to select 12 animals to be his palace guards. He wanted the Ox 🐂 to be the first for its honesty and diligence, but surprisingly, the smart Rat 🐀 covertly hid on the Ox's back and occupied the first place at the essential moment.

The Tiger 🐅 was crowned next as the King of the Forest and then the Dragon 🐉 as the Lord of the Sea. However, the Rabbit 🐇 won a race against the Dragon and gained the No. 4 spot. The Snake 🐍, Horse 🐎, Sheep 🐑, Monkey 🐒, and Rooster 🐓 all followed. The Dog 🐕 was punished to be the last one for biting the Rabbit; but ultimately the Dog became the 11th. The 12th sign was the one animal tardy to the party, the Pig 🐖, who finally took the last place.






2022 Year of The Tiger

People born in the year of the the Tiger are brave, passionate, competitive, and charming! They can also be unpredictable, irritable, and overindulgent. Those born under this sign can make strong leaders as they are confident and can handle anything that comes along. Not one to prepare or plan, they are quick at reacting on the spot. Tigers are also known for being the center of attention and well-liked by their peers.

Famously Born in the Year of the Tiger: Lady Gaga, Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Cruise and Queen Elizabeth II.
